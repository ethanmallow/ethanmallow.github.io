Menomonee Falls is about a half hour drive to Downtown Milwaukee using I-41 US-45 South. It is also 50 minutes south of Fond du Lac if you take I-41. From Madison to Menomonee Falls is about a hour and a half drive on I-94 West. Menomonee Falls borders Germantown to the north Brookfield to the south, and Mequon to the north east.
